## üìç REFERENCE LOCATIONS

Use these reference codes to tell me where you want to add or modify content:

### **[REF-A] TOP LEFT AREA**
- **Location:** Above PROJO heading, top-left of the screen
- **Use for:** Logo, navigation menu, top badges

---

### **[REF-B] LEFT SIDE**
- **Location:** Left side of the PROJO heading
- **Use for:** Side text, badges, icons (where WEBFLOW used to be)

---

### **[REF-C] ABOVE PROJO**
- **Location:** Directly above the PROJO heading
- **Use for:** Tagline, subtitle above the main heading

---

### **[REF-D] BELOW PROJO**
- **Location:** Directly below the PROJO heading
- **Use for:** Subtitle, tagline, description under PROJO

---

### **[REF-E] RIGHT SIDE**
- **Location:** Right side of the PROJO heading
- **Use for:** Side badges, icons (where GSAP used to be)

---

### **[REF-F] PORTAL AREA**
- **Location:** The 3D portal/scene section
- **Use for:** Changes to the portal itself, overlays on portal

---

### **[REF-G] BOTTOM SECTION**
- **Location:** Below the portal, general bottom area
- **Use for:** Call-to-action buttons, large bottom text

---

### **[REF-H] BOTTOM LEFT TEXT AREA**
- **Location:** Bottom-left text area
- **Use for:** Left-aligned description text

---

### **[REF-I] BOTTOM RIGHT TEXT AREA**
- **Location:** Bottom-right text area  
- **Use for:** Right-aligned text, call-to-action

---

### **[REF-J] VERY BOTTOM**
- **Location:** At the very bottom of the hero section
- **Use for:** Scroll indicators, bottom navigation
